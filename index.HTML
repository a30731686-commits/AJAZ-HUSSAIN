About Ajaz Hussain

Ajaz Hussain is an innovation-driven professional associated with PPDC under the Ministry of MSME, Government of India. He works toward strengthening the Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME) ecosystem by promoting technology adoption, industrial modernization, and entrepreneurial capacity building. Professional Role At PPDC, Ajaz Hussain contributes to: Supporting MSMEs in improving productivity and competitiveness Guiding startups and early-stage entrepreneurs Promoting innovation-led growth strategies Encouraging digital transformation and modernization in manufacturing and service sectors Areas of Expertise Innovation Strategy & Development Startup Mentorship & Advisory MSME Growth & Industrial Support Technology Enablement & Digital Adoption Entrepreneur Development Programs
